Guidance on product information requirements

Description of the Manufacturing Process of the Product – Information requested

  1. Amounts of reagents used including additives and catalysts
  2. Details and specification of reagents, including Material Safety Data Sheets (MSDSs)
  3. Description of the manufacturing process - order of additions & details of important process parameters
  4. The chemical/physical reactions and any side reactions
  5. Details of any product finishing processes
  6. Any other relevant information

Technical Specification for Each Component

  1. You are responsible for providing complete detailed information of ALL components of the product formulation, including ALL constituents of each primary component that has been listed in Section 6.1.
  2. Applicants may need to contact their suppliers to obtain this information.
  3. DWI does not enter into confidentiality agreements but all product information is treated in confidence and it is used for the purposes of assessing this application for approval only. No product information is released to a third party without the authorisation of the manufacturer – see http://www.dwi.gov.uk/drinking-water-products/faqs/FAQ3.pdf.
  4. Suppliers may send information direct to the DWI – see appropriate letters on the website - http://www.dwi.gov.uk/drinking-water-products/advice-and-approval/forms/letter.doc.
  5. This information must be in ENGLISH and quoting the relevant applicant, product names and DWI reference number so that the information can be linked to the correct application for approval.

Product Specific Information – Polyamines

For POLYAMINES please indicate the residual amounts of:

  • epichlorohydrin
  • 1,3-dichloropropanol
  • 2,3- dichloropropanol
  • 3-monochloropropandiol

and any other chloropropanols present in batches of the product.

This information should be given as (X±Y) ppm where X is the mean value and Y is the measurement of uncertainty.

Any other information relating to the application for approval of your product, not requested elsewhere on this form, should be provided along with this form and all other information requested.
